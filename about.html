About Us

Royal Stone CounterTops is a locally owned and operated company proudly serving Winnipeg and the surrounding areas. We specialize in the design, fabrication, and installation of high-quality stone countertops for kitchens, islands, bathrooms, and more.

Our mission is simple: to bring lasting beauty and functionality to your home with custom-crafted countertops that match your style, needs, and budget.

Whether you're renovating, building new, or upgrading your space, we offer a wide selection of premium granite, quartz, and marble slabs—each carefully selected for durability and elegance.

Why Choose Royal Stone?

  • Expert Craftsmanship - Our team brings years of experience and attention to detail to every project.
  • Wide Selection of Stone - Explore a range of colors and finishes to match any interior design.
  • Locally Trusted - We're proud to work with homeowners, contractors, and designers across Winnipeg.
  • Free Quotes & Personalized Service - From concept to installation, we're with you every step of the way.

At Royal Stone CounterTops, we believe every surface should reflect the character of the space—and the people who live in it.
